Smart Garden Engineering Project

Alpha Gamma Class of Theta Tau

Why a Smart Garden?

With the pandemic, we have all picked up new hobbies to keep us busy and less bored. Some of us have attempted to garden and plant, but only faced failure and plant death. We thought it would be a great idea to build a smart garden that would water, light, and track growth itself. Additionally, we thought it would be a great project that combined each of our skillsets or pushed us out of our comfort zones.
